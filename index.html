How to play

 Bonjour!
 Your task is to find a hidden 4-digit number. Input your guess and see the feedback:
 Green square: Correct number, but wrong position.
 Yellow square: Correct number in the correct position.
 Modes:
 Easy mode: 4 unique digits, 5 attempts, 10 points deduction per  wrong guess.
 Hard mode: 4 digits (may include duplicates), 10 attempts, you will only receive feedback on how many numbers you guessed correctly and how many of them are in the right positions increasing points deduction for each wrong guess.
 Best of luck! Have fun!
